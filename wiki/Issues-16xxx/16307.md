Fix small bug in G.strong_orientation() by replacing 
`d.has_edge(e[0].e[1]) ` 
with 
`d.has_edge(e[0],e[1])`

The bug causes G.strong_orientation() to fail for G a multigraph.  Also added a small multigraph to the doctests for G.strong_orientation()
