Evaluation of e.g. `elliptic_f(asin((-b<sup>(1/3)*(c+d*x)</sup>(1/3)+(b*c-a*d)<sup>(1/3)*(1+sqrt(3)))/(-b</sup>(1/3)*(c+d*x)<sup>(1/3)+(b*c-a*d)</sup>(1/3)*(1-sqrt(3)))),-7+4*sqrt(3))` by Sage takes surprising 9 sec on a fast system, although no computation should be involved (in==out).
